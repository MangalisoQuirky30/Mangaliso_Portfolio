About Me

My name is Mangaliso Sereo(21) and I love cameras. I am highly skilled both infront and behind the camera. That includes modeling, photography and directing. I have been a model since my first picture as a child, or at least, that's what I tell myself. As I was growing up, I got exposed to actual modeling and photography. Overtime, I understood the industry better and acquired a skill on how I can bring my own creative ideas to life, that's how I became a director. I direct everything, from photoshoots to shooting music videos. I even have a reality TV show, and guess who shoots it. That's right, me.

Model

I have always been photogenic and never scared of the camera. I guess you could say I was born a model. It was only in 2011 that I got exposed to real modeling. I created my first modeling profile at Kids2000 at the age of 14. I could say, I wasted two years worth of a contract, because I was clueless on how things worked. I signed up withanother agency at a later stage and that is where I had my first photoshoot, and as you can already imagine, I killed itðŸ”¥

That is when I became aware that I really enjoyed being infront of the camera. Fast forward, five years later, I have done a lot of photoshoots with both big and small (upcoming/local) brands all over Cape Town. Currently, I am working on my profile, I want to freelance and do photoshoots as I please and get 100% of the cheque while at it.

Photographer

I became so good at modeling and had so much experience that I knew the best way to take pictures and would not stop taking pictures until I got the perfect shots. It got to a point where I would direct the photographer myself, which included angles, what and what not to show on the frames.

I didn't know I had a good eye for taking pictures until the cameraman took a break and I decided to snap some one's and two's. I fell in love on the spot. I didn't even need anyone to approve my shots, I knew exactly how to impress myself. I took the pictures the exact same way I had imagined it before taking the pictures, visions. Only then did it start to make sense why I acted like a perfectionist whenever someone else took pictures.

Director

Because of the modeling and photography skills, and experience that I acquired over the years, I was well equipped to direct either models or photographers, sometimes both. It started as a joke at first, early 2018, I was doing it for free during a friend's shoot. She felt awkward and the photographer was running out of idea. So, I took over. I know this might sound too good to be true, but it was a hit. Photographers and brands have been booking me to direct their photoshoots ever since.
